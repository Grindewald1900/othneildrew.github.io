About Othneil

Othneil is highly creative visionary with a strong background in UI/UX design, front-end/back-end development, strategic planning/marketing, and technical writing.

Ever since his first flight in the eighth grade, he has been passionate about aviation and science. Drew has advanced skills in responsive web design and web development for major projects and companies and has developed an understanding of strategic business planning through his work with Student Pilot Portal. Drew prides himself on his ability to collaborate well with teams in any environment.

Drew serves as an advocate for promoting youth in the aviation industry and is actively involved with many organizations like the Organization of Black Aerospace Professions, Virgin Islands Chapter Tuskegee Airmen, Inc. (VICTAI), U.S. Dream Academy, and the United States Coast Guard Auxiliary. He has raised more than $2,000 in donations for nonprofits through effective fundraising efforts, while volunteering 900+ hours of service.

Interests: Flight Simulation, Photography, Gaming, Guitar, NCIS: Lost Angeles
